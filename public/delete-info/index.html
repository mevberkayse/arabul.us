How to Delete Your Account

Follow these steps to delete your account:

  1. Log in to your account using your username and password.
  2. Navigate to the Account Settings section.
  3. Scroll down to the Delete Account option.
  4. Click the Delete Account button.
  5. Read the confirmation message carefully and confirm your choice.
  6. You may be asked to re-enter your password to confirm.
  7. Click Confirm to complete the deletion process.

Note: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. Ensure you have saved any necessary data before proceeding.

If you encounter any issues, please contact support.
